Features

Problem

Independent creators and small studios often struggle to unlock capital from future revenue because traditional lenders do not underwrite intellectual property well. At the same time, investors want access to new, differentiated asset classes with real cash-flow backing, not just speculative startup bets.

Solution

We are building a marketplace that helps verified creators raise funding against future royalty streams from songs, films, and other IP assets. Investors participate through contract-based royalty-sharing deals with clear disclosures, limited access, and structured payouts, rather than public stock-like trading.

How It Works

Creators submit proof of rights, projected royalty income, and funding requirements. The platform verifies ownership, standardizes deal terms, connects the project to eligible investors, and routes collections through controlled payment flows so investors receive their agreed share over time.

Why Now

India’s creator economy is growing, but IP financing remains underdeveloped. At the same time, public retail equity crowdfunding faces major regulatory limits, while private placement structures remain the most realistic path for compliant IP-backed fundraising.

Compliance Positioning

The platform will operate as a private, invite-only funding marketplace with strict KYC, investor eligibility checks, disclosure documents, and legal review of each transaction. It will not market itself as a public stock exchange or open retail crowdfunding app, since public solicitation and unregulated securities-like offerings can create regulatory issues in India.
